Welcome to the website documenting Marianopolis' Mission to Earth.

I am Dr. Nefario and I have led the select students of intergalactic Marianopolis throughout the past three months. Our mission is to travel to Earth and explore the unknowns of a nuclearly destroyed planet.

To prepare for this mission, the Cadets have designed and built a robot to explore areas toxic to humans, filmed a documentary that will be updated throughout the mission and a website to inform people like you about our efforts and built their very own space pod to travel in.

Please take your time while browsing this website and keep in mind the hard work the Cadets put into this mission.
