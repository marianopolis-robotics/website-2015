Marianopolis College is an English-language private CEGEP currently located in Westmount, Quebec, Canada, and is one of the smallest Anglophone colleges of the province, giving students the personal attention they need to succeed.

Recognized as one of Quebec's top pre-university colleges, Marianopolis College boasts a diverse student body comprised of 2,000 students from over 175 public and private high schools, English and French, from Quebec and abroad.

From early admission to Harvard University to direct entry to McGill University's Faculty of Law, Marianopolis College's students gain entry to the world's top universities every year.

It is also the only college in Quebec that offers five distinct special certificates, which allow students to touch upon other interests outside their curriculum: Environmental Studies, International Studies, Law and Social Justice, Native Studies and Third World Studies.

Student Life

Student life at Marianopolis is supported by Congress, the student government, which organizes a variety of events and activities such as Halloween, Christmas, Earth Week, Valentine's Day, etc. The College has currently up to 100 student clubs and several athletic teams involved in competitive sports such as volleyball, badminton, dragon boat, etc. Marianopolis also offers a gymnasium and fitness center for students during the week.

Marianopolis is also the only college to hold a universal lunch break, called Activity Period, twice a week. This common break is an opportunity for students to hear from world-renowned guest speakers, play intramural sports, participate in clubs activities and socialize with peers outside of class.

Students also have the opportunity to get involved in a variety of enriching extracurricular activities, from the annual ArtsFest and the Christmas Fair Trade Bazaar, to creative writing, math and robotics competitions.

Why Marianopolis?

Marianopolis is the place for motivated young people to achieve, grow and succeed. As Quebec's only English-language private college, Marianopolis has been uniquely positioned for more than a century to offer unparalleled support to students from various backgrounds. The college also provides exceptional resources and services to students in the classroom and beyond to guarantee a most empowering academic experience. At Marianopolis, students are challenged and prepared to take on the final step, university.
